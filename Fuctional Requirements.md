# ChessProject Functional Requirements

The following functional requirements have been taking from the [instructions](INSTRUCTIONS.md).

## Chessboard

1. A chessboard is an 8 x 8 grid (with a 0 index)

## Pieces

1. Pieces can be either black or white
1. Pieces have 2 actions, move and capture

### Pawns

1. Pawns can only move forward
1. Pawns can only move one space
1. Pawns can move 2 spaces on their first move (stretch goal)
1. Pawns can only capture in a forward diagonal direction (to be implemented in the next sprint)
